## Amazon Sales Data Analysis Dashboard
## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive sales analytics dashboard developed to analyze order quantity, sales performance, and logistics insights from an e-commerce dataset. The dashboard highlights key metrics such as total products sold, total revenue, shipping performance, category demand, and regional distribution of orders.

The objective of this project is to extract meaningful business insights that can help stakeholders understand sales trends, customer demand, product performance, and operational efficiency

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional sales information from an e-commerce platform and includes the following fields:
- Order ID
- Date / Week of Order
- Product Category
- Product Size
- Quantity Sold
- Order Status (Shipped, Cancelled, Returned, etc.)
- Courier Service Level (Standard / Expedited)
- Sales Channel (Amazon / Non-Amazon)
- B2B Indicator
- State / Region
- Total Sales Amount

## Dashboard Features
## Sales Distribution by State
A geographical visualization highlights state-wise order quantities across India, helping identify high-demand regions.

## Top States by Quantity and Shipping Service
Shows the top 10 states by quantity sold and compares expedited vs standard shipping performance

## Courier Service Status Analysis
Breakdown of order fulfillment status including:

- Shipped
- Delivered
- Cancelled
- Returned
- Pending
This helps evaluate logistics efficiency and delivery performance.

## Sales Channel Analysis
Compares sales quantity between:
- Amazon channel
- Non-Amazon channels
Findings show the majority of orders originate from Amazon

## B2B vs B2C Sales
The dashboard distinguishes between B2B and non-B2B sales, revealing that the business primarily operates in B2C retail transactions.

## Weekly Sales Trends
Tracks quantity and revenue trends over time to identify:

- Peak sales periods
- Seasonal fluctuations
- Category performance trends

## Product Size and Category Analysis
Analyzes demand by:
- Clothing sizes (XS – 6XL)
- Product categories (Kurta, Saree, Set, Western Dress, etc.)
This helps identify popular sizes and categories for inventory planning.

## Key Insights
- Maharashtra and Karnataka contribute the highest order volumes.
- Expedited shipping is frequently used in high-demand states.
- 94% of orders are successfully shipped, indicating strong logistics performance.
- Amazon channel dominates sales, contributing nearly all transactions.
- B2B sales are minimal, meaning the business mainly serves individual consumers.
- Medium and Large sizes show the highest demand among apparel products.
- Weekly sales trends show strong activity in April and early May, followed by gradual stabilization.
